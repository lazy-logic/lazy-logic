## About

I build the whole thing, from the agent that makes a decision to the interface a customer touches to the container it runs in. Lately that has meant AI customer service for insurance, autonomous product strategy agents, WhatsApp onboarding for a lease to own bike programme, and a browser based softphone and CRM for teams across Ghana. I work in the open where I can and keep client work private where I should.

## What I work on

- AI agents that do real work: research, decision support, customer service, product strategy
- Retrieval augmented generation and language model applications wired into live systems
- Full stack SaaS: Next.js and React on the front, FastAPI and Node on the back, Postgres underneath
- WhatsApp, voice, and SMS automation for businesses that live on those channels
- Data and monitoring tools, several of them focused on problems specific to Ghana

## A few things I have built

**MOOVON Insure Intelligence**
An AI customer service assistant and a real time operations dashboard for an insurance premium finance business.

**Autonomous Product Strategy Agent**
An agent that takes a high level intent and produces a rigorous, data backed market requirements document, the way a technical product manager would.

**Augvox**
A browser based softphone with a CRM and an omnichannel inbox for WhatsApp and SMS, built for teams across Ghana.

**Gogo eBikes**
A lease to own electric bike programme with a WhatsApp bot that onboards riders end to end, and a dashboard behind it.

**OFWA Galamsey Monitor**
A tool that reads illegal mining site data in Ghana, runs the analysis, stores it, and serves the results over an API.
